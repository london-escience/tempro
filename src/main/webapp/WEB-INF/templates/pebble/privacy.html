Privacy Policy

The TemPSS tool collects and stores some information when you use it. Here we describe what data is collected and stored and why:

  • TemPSS may store cookies (small pieces of information stored by your web browser) to provide some of the functionality of the website and to help improve your experience of using TemPSS.
  • TemPSS stores the information that you provide when you register with the site. Passwords are stored in an encrypted form.
  • TemPSS may collect usage statistics to help us understand how users are using the site and to help us improve the usability and user experience of TemPSS.
  • TemPSS stores input data that you upload (e.g. geometry files) for creating Nektar++ input files.
